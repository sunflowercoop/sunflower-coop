Annual meeting minutes
Hi folks we had quite a dramatic annual meeting; former members of the co-op (now board members) told us that the co-op was being dissolved and would be sold to current (Generation 2) members. Generation 1 members are those of you who lived here between 1980 and 2005. Generation 2 members are those of us who have lived here since 2005 (the year that the mortgage was paid off). In establishing the co-op, it was originally planned that the co-op would be sold by the original members so that they could divest their responsibility of the co-op ultimately and that others would assume that responsibility. Dave Hooper, Jim Hoadley and Michael Owens are now working on that so do get in contact with them to assist them with that. Generation 2 members - get in contact with sunflower directly at sunflower_coop@yahoo.com if you want to be involved in establishing the new co-op moving forwards. These are exciting times and a ripe opportunity for you to give back to the household that gave you community, connectivity and a rich teaching in co-operative and collaborative living (how's that for alliteration!!) We plan on establishing a co-op which will be strong and resilient, and have already put in place a budget committee, by-laws committee and legal committee. We need legal assistance and also want to make this a GREEN co-op. All your ideas and involvement are most welcome. You can also get in touch with us to be involved as a board member on 447 1268. We'll post more here as things develop. For now, the Gen 1 board (Transition/ Dissolution board) are working on sorting out the finances to make a smooth transition. Love from Sunflower! Michele Hallahan (Budget committee)
